The Fire Weather Index (FWI) System is a comprehensive meteorological tool designed to assess fire danger based on weather conditions. Our advanced machine learning model analyzes nine critical parameters including temperature, relative humidity, wind speed, rainfall, and specialized fire weather codes (FFMC, DMC, ISI) to provide accurate fire weather risk predictions for different geographical regions.
